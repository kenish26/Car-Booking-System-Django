Our Mission

We’re on a mission to empower people - for them to reach anywhere and anytime, by designing a platform that helps find, reserve and pay for parking easily. We aim to make parking hassle free, Indian cities more accessible and people more connected.

About our Park-IT Team

All of us have travelled different paths in life but have come together for a typical cause – not to get our vehicles towed. We at Park-IT share a common goal - to make parking easier like never before through India’s first online parking booking app. Solving this problem in India is no easy feat as almost everyone in the country is struggling with the ever-increasing parking issue. We are a team of creative thinkers, innovators and change-seekers who have assembled together and are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of people and you are cordially invited to be a part of it.
